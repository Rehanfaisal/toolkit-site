Questions, answered

Is this affiliated with itch.io?

No. Itch Toolkit is an independent tool built by a fellow itch.io creator, and is not endorsed by or affiliated with itch corp. It works on top of your own logged-in account and never asks for your itch.io password.

Where does my data go?

Your analytics are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. Only your account email and the settings you choose to sync are stored. Full details in the privacy policy.

Do I need Pro to use it?

No — the analytics dashboard, the core of the toolkit, is completely free with no time limit. Pro adds the growth tools: alerts, tag research, indexing monitoring, AI suggestions and exports.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, one click. You keep Pro until the end of the period you paid for. Payments are handled by Paddle, our merchant of record.
