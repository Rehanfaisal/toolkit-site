Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

The short version: your itch.io analytics data is processed locally in your browser and is not uploaded to our servers. We store only your account email and the small settings you choose to sync. We never sell data. Payments are handled by Paddle — we never see your card details.

1. Who we are

Itch Toolkit is operated by Rehan Faisal ("RehanDev"). Contact: rehanfaisal123@gmail.com. Itch Toolkit is not affiliated with itch corp / itch.io.

3. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data. Ever.
  • We do not upload your itch.io analytics or revenue figures to our servers.
  • We do not read or store your itch.io password — the extension uses your existing logged-in session and your itch.io API key, which stays in your local extension storage (synced only if you enable cloud sync).
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Legal basis & retention

We process account data to provide the service you signed up for (performance of contract) and minimal analytics on this website based on legitimate interest. Account and sync data are kept while your account exists and deleted when you delete your account or on request. Local extension data is removed when you uninstall the extension or clear its storage.

5. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing rehanfaisal123@gmail.com from your account email. Deleting your account removes your synced data from Firestore. Depending on where you live (e.g. EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA), you may have additional statutory rights, which we honour.

6. Third-party processors

  • Paddle.com — payments, invoicing and tax (their privacy policy)
  • Google Firebase / Firestore — authentication and settings sync
  • Google Gemini API — on-demand AI suggestions
  • Google Analytics — website traffic statistics (website only, not the extension)

7. Security

Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS). Sync data is protected by Firebase security rules so only your authenticated account can read it. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we minimise what we collect in the first place — the most sensitive data (your analytics) never leaves your browser.

8. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes

We will post any changes to this policy on this page with an updated date, and note material changes on the extension's dashboard.
